methodology

Testing Process

Structured, repeatable process aligned with real-world adversary techniques.

  • Clarify objectives, in-scope assets, constraints, and success criteria, then map systems and trust boundaries.

  • Gather OSINT, fingerprint technologies, and enumerate services, endpoints, and exposed interfaces.

  • Identify high-value assets and plausible abuse paths to prioritize depth of testing.

  • Combine targeted manual testing with curated tooling for broad and deep coverage across all attack surfaces.

  • Safely exploit issues to prove impact while protecting availability and data integrity throughout the engagement.

  • Score findings with CVSS 3.1 and business context so teams can prioritize remediation effectively.

  • Deliver structured reports with clear reproduction steps, OWASP mapping, impact rating, and pragmatic fixes.
